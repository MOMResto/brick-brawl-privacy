BRICK BRAWL — Delete Account

How to delete your account

  1. Send an email to support@momresto.com
  2. Subject line: Delete My BRICK BRAWL Account
  3. Include your username or email address used to register
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

What data will be deleted

  • Your account (username, email, password hash)
  • Your profile (display name, rank, level, XP, coins)
  • Your game statistics (games played, wins, scores)
  • Your match history
  • Your clan membership
  • Your chat messages
  • Your friend connections
  • Your push notification tokens

What data may be retained

  • Anonymized aggregate statistics (total games played globally)
  • Server logs may be retained for up to 90 days for security purposes

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Contact: support@momresto.com
