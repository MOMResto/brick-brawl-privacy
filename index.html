BRICK BRAWL — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Developer: MOM

1. Information We Collect

Account Data: When you create an account, we collect your username, email address, and encrypted password. If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google profile name and email.

Game Data: We collect gameplay statistics (scores, levels completed, achievements, match history), clan membership, and chat messages you send within clans.

Device Data: We may collect device identifiers for push notifications (FCM token) and advertising purposes (AdMob).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game service
  • To manage your account and authenticate you
  • To display leaderboards and match history
  • To enable social features (friends, clans, chat)
  • To show personalized advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To improve game performance and fix bugs

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Displays ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In: Used for authentication. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Railway (Hosting): Our server is hosted on Railway. Data is stored on their infrastructure.
  • Hostinger (Database): Game data is stored in a MySQL database hosted by Hostinger.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. API communication uses HTTPS encryption. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

BRICK BRAWL is rated for Everyone. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Object: Opt out of personalized advertising via your device's ad settings or by contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
